: Berlin Rent Price Changes (2008–2022) :

This map visualizes the changes in rent prices across Berlin's city quarters between 2008 and 2022, focusing on the net cold rent per square meter (EUR/m²) [1]. By comparing the rental data from these two years, the map highlights areas that have experienced the most significant rent hikes and those with the least change. The data is categorized using the Natural Breaks (Jenks) classification method, which optimally groups the values based on the distribution of the data, allowing for a clear understanding of rent trends in different neighborhoods. The analysis provides insights into how rent prices have evolved over time, shedding light on areas with rapid gentrification as well as those with relatively stable rents.

This should give your map viewers a solid understanding of the purpose and methodology behind your analysis.
